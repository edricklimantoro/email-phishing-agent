# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Security analysts monitoring email phishing detection in real-time. They scan flagged emails, review classification confidence scores, and track threat trends across the organization. Context: high-stakes monitoring, often under time pressure, scanning for anomalies.

## Product Purpose

Surface phishing email detection results from a NeMo Guardrails + LLM pipeline. The dashboard provides real-time visibility into email classifications (safe, phishing, security violation), guardrail activity, and per-email detail for investigation. Success means analysts trust the system and catch threats faster.

## Brand Personality

Precise, sharp, clinical. Calm authority under pressure. Not playful, not aggressive. The interface should feel like a well-calibrated instrument: clear readouts, minimal noise, immediate comprehension.

## Anti-references

- Not neon-cyber: no glowing borders, matrix rain, or hacker aesthetic
- Not enterprise-grey: no corporate blandness, no soulless utilitarianism
- Not SaaS-cream: no soft gradients, friendly illustrations, or pastel defaults
- Balance between generic SaaS polish and enterprise seriousness

## Design Principles

1. **Clarity over decoration**: Every pixel serves comprehension. No visual noise.
2. **Status at a glance**: Threat level readable in under 2 seconds from across the room.
3. **Progressive disclosure**: Summary first, detail on demand. Don't overwhelm.
4. **Earned trust**: Consistent, predictable, reliable. No surprises in a security tool.
5. **Dark by context**: Security analysts work in dim SOC environments. Dark theme is functional, not aesthetic.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA compliance. Keyboard navigation throughout. Screen reader support with ARIA labels. Color is never the sole indicator of status (always paired with text/icons). Reduced motion support via `prefers-reduced-motion`.
